We are Millions

Role: Art Director and coordinator 
‚ÄúWe Are Millions‚Äù in defense of press freedom and Julian Assange in collaboration with Courage Foundation (ref: Nathan Fuller)

Visual language is defined to emphasize individual portraits and their facial expressions while displaying cross-border solidarity. We Are millions displays a diverse multitude of people. In this piece, ordinary folk and very prominent cultural, artistic and political figures are mixed together through portraits of themselves.

This participative art piece appeared as an exhibition around Europe and beyond. Some exhibitions and events appeared spontaneously with local initiatives.

Island School of Social Autonomy

Role: Co-founder, designer, web developer...

The Island School of Social Autonomy (ISSA) is a place that imagines, experiments with, and cultivates forms of knowledge production and sharing that go beyond traditional notions of education and its purpose. We perceive social autonomy as the ability of individuals to function as cooperative group members, engaging in communal self-governance while being aware of the interconnectedness and interdependence of communities within broader networks (or archipelagoes) of human and non-human life-organization.
