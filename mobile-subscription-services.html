Mobile Subscription Services

Created:
Designing identity, user journey and friendlier forms for PlussMobil
Client:
2021

PlussMobil is part of Aller Media, one of Norway's largest media houses. PlussMobil rents a mobile network from Telenor and sells subscription services to Aller's  audience.

Subscription

Project challenge, was to convert visitor to subscriber while having to fill in long and complex steps (fixed decision) by providing more pleasant user interface and experience. These ware the foundation principles: 

  • Form steps to encourage user to finish the journey 
  • Using Emojies to make our forms more fun 🤙👌👏❤️
  • Revealing form sections as they become relevant, not to scare subscriber
  • Great mobile experience with cozy touch targets
