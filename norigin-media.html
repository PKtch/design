Visual language for a modern tech company should emphasizes clean and sharp components, including the logo, web and interface design. Styled letter N is creating distinct and lovable brand experience for all platforms and touch points.
This approach creates an identity that never interferes with the medium, but at the same time makes a strong distinctive character that stands out.
